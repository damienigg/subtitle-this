How to subtitle a film

Two manual flows from the UI — Subtitle This never auto-translates anything on its own:

  1. Per item: open Library, find the item you want, click Subtitle this. The job appears below in the jobs list.
  2. Whole library: hit Sweep library above. One job is queued for every item missing a subtitle in your default target language ({{ settings.default_target_lang }}). Already-subtitled items are skipped.
